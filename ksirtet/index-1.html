1. Introduction

This program is a clone of the well known game Tetris. You must fit the falling pieces (by rotation and translation) to form full lines. A line is destroyed when all free spaces are filled. Destroying multiple lines at the same time gives you more points. A four-line destruction is called a "tetris" : it gives a maximum amount of points.

The multiplayer option is in the alpha stage, so expect it to be buggy. You can play with 2 or more players. When destroying a line, a player sends debris at the bottom of his neighbour's game.
