1. Introduction

KMines is the classical Minesweeper game.

You must discover all the cases without a mine to win:

  • Left-button will uncover a case.
  • Right-button will mark a case as containing a mine or as being uncertain.
  • Middle-button will try to clear the eigth surrounding cases if there are the right number of cases already marked as containing a mine.
  • Pressing the smiley will start a new game.
